Doing something right - Jan 17, 2017

I came across a new text editor called Atom .
It's pretty good. I was using Sublime Text 3 before but I didn't like how it was quite bare-boned.

Anyways, I had been intrigued since picking up Web Development and was going around all of my usual websites to check out their source codes and comparing them to mine to see how they differ.
I had seen some more 'sophisticated' source codes and was wondering if what I was doing was pretty 'low class'.
But apparently, not the case!
